Drops

a poem

scroll
Maybe just a little more And I would have touched the shore The waves were still chanting my name But sea no longer felt the same
I chose a path that felt secure stepped back from what I adore A decision that felt more mature Yet left my heart forever unsure
Strangely it felt peaceful there Far away from storm and fear But soon enough the silence met The falling cold drops of regret
I stood unanswered, it started to rain Even the thunder was roaring with pain Those clouds that seemed fully content Were too shedding drops of lament
Then they stopped and moved forward As if they started chasing the next bird Is it that their scars were gone, Or that they learned to carry on?
